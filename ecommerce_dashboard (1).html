📊 Performance Highlights
🏆
Best Category — Electronics
Electronics dominates with ₹16.5M revenue — 53.8% of total sales across 320 orders. No other category comes close.
→ Increase inventory, bundle accessories, upsell warranties on every device purchase.
📅
Best Month — November 2025
Nov 2025 peaked at ₹24.6L — 60% above the 24-month average. Festive demand clearly drives extreme spikes.
→ Pre-load stock from September. Launch early-bird sales 3 weeks before Diwali.
🛍️
Top Product — Laptop
Laptop generated ₹83.7L (61 orders, 104 units) — highest single-SKU revenue in the entire catalog.
→ Create EMI & trade-in programs. Bundle laptop bags, mice and insurance to lift AOV.
👤 Customer Intelligence
⭐
Top Spender — Rudra Kulkarni
Single customer spent ₹4.99L in one order — confirms an extreme high-value segment that needs dedicated treatment.
→ Launch VIP programme for LTV > ₹2L. Assign key account managers to top 50 customers.
🔄
Low Repeat Rate
Most customers placed only 1 order across 2 years. Acquisition cost is being wasted without retention strategy.
→ Subscription boxes, 14-day re-engagement email flows, and cashback loyalty points.
📊
Average Order Value: ₹12,248
High AOV confirms electronics dominance but also means most customers are one-time high-value buyers.
→ Push smaller category bundles (Fashion + Beauty) to increase order frequency.
📍 Geographic Insights
🗺️
Top State — Maharashtra (₹45.6L)
Maharashtra + Gujarat together account for 27% of total revenue. Metro cities like Nashik and Pune are key growth drivers.
→ Open fulfilment centre in Pune for next-day delivery in Maharashtra + Gujarat.
🌍
Untapped — Central & North-East
Central and North-East zones contribute under 8% of revenue despite large population bases and growing internet penetration.
→ Vernacular ads, COD-first strategy, and regional influencer partnerships to unlock these zones.
📈 Growth & Margins
🚀
+17% YoY Growth
2025 estimated at ₹18.0M vs 2024's ₹15.6M — strong consistent growth trend across all categories.
→ Maintain Q4 2025 momentum. Heavy discounting in Nov + loyalty reactivation to hit ₹20M target.
💹
18.5% Profit Margin
Healthy margin, but cross-sell potential is massively underutilised. Every electronics order is an accessories opportunity.
→ Force-bundle high-margin accessories (covers, cables, warranties) into checkout flow.
📉
Underperforming — Books & Beauty
Books (₹1.42L) and Beauty (₹3.42L) trail far behind. Both categories have high potential but low current investment.
→ Curated book boxes + Beauty sampling kits via Instagram reels to drive discovery.
